        mDay welcomes you to mDay Homes!
        mDay is a novelty idea that is designed with immigrants in mind and intended for their comforts. Often, people live away from home. Home do not live away from them. Their obligation to reconnect to their roots is a basis of their existence in any corner of the world they find themselves.
          To this end, they depend on loved ones who often than not betray them and divert their hard-earned funds into private use.
         mDay exists to bridge this gap. We carry out a comprehensive and professional exploration of the home or estate you want to build, We give solid and picturesque representation of your object of interests, we fund your project on your behalf. You pay us at your convenience.  We represent your interests on your behalf as if you are there in person. Fill the form and indicate what you want to do in Nigeria and we will send you options to choose from. We feel good only when you are satisfied!                                                                                          By Sun Afolabi,  West Texas A&M University Project
